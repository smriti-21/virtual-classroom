What do Virtuality Courses include ?

Each Virtuality course is created, owned and managed by the instructor(s). The foundation
of each Virtuality course are its lectures, which can include videos, slides, text and additional resources
the instructor has uploaded. In addition, instructors can add quizzes, practice tests, assignments and
coding exercises, as a way to enhance the learning experience of students.

How do I take a Virtuality Course ?

Virtuality courses are entirely on-demand, which means that you can enroll in the courses that interest
you and learn at your own pace.
You can begin the course whenever you like, and there are no deadlines to complete it.
Virtuality courses can be accessed from several different devices and platforms, including a desktop,
laptop, and our Android or iOS mobile app.
Detailed information on Virtuality’s platforms and features can be reviewed here.
After you enroll in a course, you can access it by clicking on the course link you will receive in your
confirmation email,
provided you’re logged into your Virtuality account. You can also begin the course by logging in and
navigating to your My Courses page.
For steps on how to log into your Virtuality account, please click here.

How long do I have to take the Virtuality course?

As noted above, there is no deadlines for completing the course. Even after completing the course, you
will have complete access to it.

How to request a refund?

You can quickly request a refund by contacting our support team. The request is processed and
refund is being transferred to your account within 7 days.

What about Lifetime Access?

Yes, once you have bought your course, you gain lifetime access for it, provided that your
account’s in good standing and Udemy continues to have a license to the course. So, if you wish to review
specific content in the course after you finish it, or take it all over again, you can. This scheme also applies
for Free courses.
